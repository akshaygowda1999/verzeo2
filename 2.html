Cristiano Ronaldo

International Footballer


Cristiano Ronaldo from Portugal, has exceeded expectations continuously of being a soccer player for Real Madrid C.F.
He has scored the most goals in the Champions league for a single season.
29-year old Cristiano, has inspired many football lovers and is ideal to many people.
He is proud of his accomplishments and dedicates what he has done to his father that passed away when he was young.
He is popular for his branded products including shoes, fragrance, jeans, a children's line etc. .
